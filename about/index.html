About

Built it. Now I'm building it for you.

26+ years of IT and Security experience. A multi-agent AI ecosystem. And a mission to make AI practical for small businesses across the Pacific Northwest and nationwide.

26+ years in IT & security leadership Founder, Eagle Point Publishing Pacific Northwest & nationwide

I've spent 26+ years in IT and Security. Managing infrastructure, leading security programs, and solving the kind of messy operational problems that keep businesses running.

A couple of years ago, I started experimenting with AI. Not the hype-cycle, press-release version. The practical kind. The kind that actually does the work.

I built a multi-agent AI ecosystem that runs significant parts of my personal life. Research, content creation, financial analysis, scheduling, security monitoring, task management, all handled by AI agents I designed, deployed, and manage myself. Not as a thought experiment. As production systems that run every day.

That experience taught me something important: AI isn't magic, and it's not just for big companies. The same automations that Fortune 500 firms spend millions building can be adapted for a 10-person landscaping company or a 25-seat restaurant, at a fraction of the cost.

That's what Eagle Point Publishing does. I take what I've learned building my own AI infrastructure and package it into practical, affordable services any small business owner can use.

The Mission

Eagle Point Publishing LLC exists to make AI practical for small business. Real automations, real services, priced so a Main Street shop can actually afford them. We offer both one-time builds and ongoing subscriptions, and we earn them the old-fashioned way: by making your business more profitable, more efficient, and more secure. When the AI Receptionist books another job, when the content engine fills your calendar, when the back-office automations give you your evenings back, that's the value you're paying for. Stop seeing it, and we haven't earned the next month.

Build in Public

I believe in transparency. Everything I build, I document. My newsletter, IT Matters, is often a running log of what I'm testing, what's working, and what isn't. I share the real numbers. The wins, the failures, the awkward middle stages where nothing works right yet.

Small business owners are tired of AI vendors who promise everything and explain nothing. I'd rather show you exactly how the sausage gets made and let you decide if it's worth ordering.

The Journey

How I got here.

The short version of a long career, where the lessons that shape Eagle Point Publishing actually happened.

1988 to 2000. Bachelor's in History and Associate's in Physics at Pacific Union College. Early career in hospitality and investment sales, then enlisted in the US Army as a Korean-linguist signals intelligence analyst. Three tours in South Korea working intel collection and analysis, where briefing Generals on portals I'd built myself hooked me on IT and security for good.

2000 to 2011. Federal contracting across Korea and Japan. SOC analyst at Northrop Grumman, then Director of the DISA CONUS Global SOC at CSC. Earned my Master's in Network Security along the way.

Mar 2011 to May 2013

Health Net

Director, Enterprise IT Security Architecture

Brought in to rebuild trust with the federal government after a high-profile data breach put a major VA and active-duty military healthcare contract at risk. Restored the program's standing and helped secure the contract renewal that followed.

May 2013 to Feb 2018

McAfee

Regional Sales Engineer, Commercial Northwest to Federal

Carried enterprise security portfolios across Northwest commercial accounts and later Federal. Advised CISOs and security leaders on architecture, product fit, and deployment, and learned the inside of how security vendors actually operate, which is the single most useful lens I bring into any client conversation today.

Mar 2018 to Sep 2021

Aerojet Rocketdyne

Sr. Director, Cyber & Information Security

Senior security leader for a $6B/yr aerospace and defense prime. Led a team of ~30 across Cyber Operations, Security Engineering, Vulnerability Management, and Governance, Risk, and Compliance. Modernized the security stack, stood up 24/7 monitoring and incident response, and drove DFARS and CMMC readiness across the enterprise while protecting some of the most sensitive engineering data in the industry.

Sep 2021 to Jul 2023

Momentus Space

VP IT and CISO

Ran IT, Information Security, Compliance, and the Security Operations Center for an in-space transportation startup, overseeing both internal teams and outsourced managed service providers. Expanded the company's footprint into AWS and Microsoft Azure to support space flight mission operations and digital manufacturing workflows, and optimized the administrative computing environment enough that the team put spacecraft on orbit across multiple missions while the business was still finding its feet.

Jul 2023 to Present

Vigor Marine Group

Chief Technology & Security Officer

CTSO across multiple operating companies. Delivered CMMC certification, migrated the enterprise to secure cloud, and rebuilt technology and security to match the scale of the combined business. My current day job.

Apr 2026 to Present
